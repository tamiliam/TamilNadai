Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Who We Are

Tamil Nadai is an open-source academic collaboration platform for Tamil writing standards. This platform is operated as a Free Software project under the GNU GPL licence. Contact: tamiliam@gmail.com.

What Data We Collect

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • Username — chosen by you during registration
  • Name — first and last name (optional)
  • Email address — optional, used for account recovery
  • Affiliation — e.g. your university (optional)
  • Avatar image — if you choose to upload one
  • Password — stored as a salted hash, never in plaintext

When you use the platform, we record:

  • Sentences you contribute and reviews you submit (attributed to your username)
  • Discussion posts (attributed to your username)
  • Login timestamps via standard Django session management

Why We Collect It

All data collection serves the platform's purpose: enabling collaborative review of Tamil writing conventions. We do not collect data for advertising, analytics profiling, or any commercial purpose. Contributions are attributed to usernames to maintain academic accountability in the review process.

Where Data Is Stored

Data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase (Singapore region). The application runs on Google Cloud Run (Singapore region). All data transfer uses HTTPS encryption.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your contributions (sentences, reviews, discussions) are visible to other registered members as part of the collaborative process. If the project data is published for academic purposes, personal identifiers will be removed.

Your Rights

You may:

  • View and update your profile information at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Request a copy of all data associated with your account

To exercise any of these rights, contact tamiliam@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
